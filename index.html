Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We respect your privacy. MyInstagramApp does not store, share, or misuse any personal data. We only request access to your Instagram Business account via the Instagram Graph API with your consent. No data is stored on our servers beyond your current session.

Data Collection

The only data we access is through the Instagram Graph API with your explicit permission. This may include profile information, media, and insights from your Instagram Business account. We process this data temporarily to display it to you but do not retain it after your session ends.

Cookies and Tracking

We use only essential session cookies to maintain your login session. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools that would compromise your privacy.

Data Deletion Instructions

Requesting Data Deletion

While MyInstagramApp doesn't permanently store your Instagram data, we understand you may want to ensure all temporary data is removed from our systems. Follow these simple steps:

Email support@myinstagramapp.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include your Instagram Business account username.

We will process your request within 24 hours and confirm when all temporary session data associated with your account has been removed.

What We Delete

  • Any cached Instagram data from your recent session
  • Your authentication token with the Instagram Graph API
  • Platform activity logs associated with your account

Important Note

Deleting data from MyInstagramApp does not affect your Instagram account or any data stored by Instagram/Meta. You may need to separately revoke access through your Instagram account settings if you wish to fully disconnect.
